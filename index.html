Interactive LP: Concrete & Steel Procurement

This page demonstrates a **Linear Programming** example where you must buy Concrete and Steel from two suppliers (X and Y) to meet the demand for a final product. Each product unit needs a certain amount of Concrete and Steel, and there's a limit to how much you can store on-site (all raw materials combined).

Adjust the inputs below, then click Solve LP to see the optimal purchasing plan and total cost, computed via glpk.js directly in your browser.
